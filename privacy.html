Privacy Policy

A privacy policy is a declaration or legal contract that explains how a firm or site gathers, manages, and processes client and guest data. It specifies whether the information remains private or if it is sold or shared with third parties.

Privacy Policy

We at RedMoon (RMN) (projecttotheredmoon.com) are dedicated to safeguarding any personal information we get from you. You consent to the use of the data we collect in line with this Privacy Policy if you use our services in accordance with this Privacy Policy. Your privacy is very important to us. We only collect the information necessary to provide you with a quality service. The types of processes that may lead to the collection of personal information about you are described in this policy. The goal of this Privacy Policy is to help you understand which of your personal identifying information ("PI", "Personal Information") is collected, how and when we may use it, who has access to it, and how you may remedy any mistakes in it. This notice explains our online information policies and the options you have regarding how your information is collected and used in order to better safeguard your privacy. We included this notification on our website so that it is easy to locate.

Data Gathered

We may use both automatic and manual methods, such as communications profiles and cookies, to gather some or all of the information you provide. Your name, address, telephone number, fax number, and email address, as well as dates of service, types of service, payment history, payment method, amount of payments, date of payments, domain name, and other payment information, may be included in the personal information you provide us, depending on the type of service, support, or sale inquiry you are making. All sensitive data is stored on a secure server and then transmitted. A security icon will display in your browser when you are sending personal data.

Usage of Data

This information is used for billing as well as providing service and support to our clients. We may also use this information to identify the requirements of our clients and offer them with assistance. Unauthorized access to this information is protected in every way possible. If you want to get information about your account details, you may have to present extra forms of identification. Customers can opt out of receiving RedMoon's monthly newsletter at any time by sending it to their primary contact e-mail address. We utilize IP addresses to measure access, evaluate trends, operate our site and servers, and gather broad demographic data for aggregate usage. IP addresses aren't connected to any information that can be used to identify you. The typical functionalities of IP addresses and SaaS apps may result in the inclusion of personal information about a client in the log files.

Cookies

Small text files known as "cookies" are stored in your Internet browser and include information that allows a website to identify your account. Cookies are used to remember your preferences and login information, as well as to deliver functionality that is tailored to you. Change your browser settings to refuse cookies, but be aware that this will cause some of the RedMoon website's features to be disabled. http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HTTP_cookie for further information about cookies.
